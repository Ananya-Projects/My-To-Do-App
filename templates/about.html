About My To-Do App

Welcome to My To-Do App, a simple yet powerful tool designed to help you manage your tasks efficiently. Our app allows you to add, edit, and delete tasks, set due dates, and filter tasks based on various criteria.

With an intuitive interface and customizable features, My To-Do App is here to help you stay organized and productive.

Features

  • Add and manage tasks with ease.
  • Set due dates and prioritize tasks.
  • Filter tasks by status, due date, and priority.
  • Mark tasks as done to keep track of completed items.
  • Enjoy a user-friendly interface with responsive design.
